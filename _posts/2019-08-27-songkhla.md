## Kao Kaw Saen Temple

The last is still mountains and they are close to the sea.

The tale of this place is, there is a millionaire who want to participate in merit ceremony at Wat Phra Mahathat in Nakhon Si Thammarat province. Suddenly, a storm just happened and sunken all the ships of the millionaire under the sea. His money as nine hundred thousand baht worth was sunken as well. That has been its name as ‚ÄúKao Saen‚Äù or nine hundred thousand in Thai then bend to Kao Seng until now.

It was my temple trip that has no pictures of temples. Haha. Would say I impressed the beach as it is quiet and heart-soothing.
