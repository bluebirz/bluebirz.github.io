There are tons of works and we have to add and remove code stubs here and there. How to communicate with team members where shall we have to do?

No list in paper nor tickets. We can put what to do directly in codebase with TODO comments.

---

## TODO comments

TO-DO comments are literally comments with a keyword to marking TODO items. They aren't limited to what TODO but also TOFIX or else. However, the common keyword are `TODO` and `FIXME`.

Most modern IDEs have integrated or at least have plugins in their plugin market for TODO comments. For example, Visual Studio Code has "todo-tree" plugin.
